seoul

Seoul, the capital of south korea is a city has an official 600 years history. It is famous for many reasons, it is considered as the historical ceter of the country. Seoul has many temples and four major palaces such as Gyeongbokgung, one of the most famous. The city offers many other cultural and artistic attractions, such as the Museum of Art, which features traditional porcelain, textile and jewelry exhibits, as noted by Frommer's. There are also old tombs and ancient remains such as the Seoul Fortress, located in the Jongno-gu, Jung-gu and Seongbuk-gu areas of the city.
